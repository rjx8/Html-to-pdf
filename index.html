Nikola Tesla: A Timeline of Inventions

1856 - Birth

Nikola Tesla was born on July 10, 1856, in Smiljan, Croatia.

1884 - Arrival in USA

Tesla arrived in New York City and started working with Thomas Edison.

1891 - Tesla Coil

Tesla invented the Tesla Coil, a high-voltage, high-frequency transformer.

1899 - Colorado Springs Experiments

He conducted high-voltage experiments in Colorado Springs with his magnifying transmitter.

1937 - Later Years

Tesla continued developing ideas for wireless energy and passed away in New York on January 7, 1943.
